Executive Summary

Boxxed is a logistics and fintech infrastructure platform enabling SME exporters to manage cross-border shipping, payments, and operations through a unified system.

Problem

SME exporters rely on fragmented WhatsApp-based logistics coordination, resulting in inefficiencies, inconsistent pricing, and lack of operational visibility.

Solution

A unified platform for booking shipments, managing payments, tracking logistics, and maintaining business operations.

Monetization

Multi-layer model: logistics margins, business tools, bill payments, and loyalty system.

Competitive Advantage

Hybrid execution + platform model with SME niche focus, embedded fintech (wallet + KYC), and loyalty-driven retention system.

Go-To-Market

Niche SME communities, WhatsApp referrals, content-led acquisition funnels, and loyalty-driven growth loops.
